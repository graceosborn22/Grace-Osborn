i had a dream you found a new girlfriend
in the used bookstore
we walked inside together
i came out alone

to pluck a volume off the shelf
but when i came back
you had grown old and gray with someone else

me still in my young, thin body
littered with acne scars
mouth agape
when i saw her wrinkly hand in yours

when your eyes met mine
they twinkled
as if tickling an old memory
“well”
you explained,
and i could tell your heart was breaking
“you had been gone so long
i had to move on
i had to stop searching”
